# Lesson: Creating Your Study Plan

## Key Purpose:
- Build a structured study strategy based on your knowledge gaps

## How the Course Works:
- Each module includes diagnostic questions
- Questions are based on real-world scenarios (Cymbal Superstore)
- Goal is to identify weak areas, not to get everything correct

## Important Concepts:
- You are NOT expected to know everything at the start
- This is a beginner-phase course in your cloud journey
- Mistakes are part of the learning process

## Study Strategy:
- Use diagnostic questions to find gaps
- Take notes on confusing topics
- Use provided resources to improve weak areas

## Resources Provided:
- Google Cloud documentation
- Courses and modules
- Skill badges (hands-on labs)
- Practice questions

## Workbook Usage:
- Track concepts you don’t understand
- Highlight resources to revisit
- Build a personalized study plan

## Weekly Planning:
- Create weekly goals and study activities
- Focus on consistent progress over perfection

## Key Takeaway:
- Your study plan should be guided by your weaknesses, not just by completing content.
